|| General response example: <Patient> I've been experiencing chronic heartburn, even after trying antacids and dietary changes. What can I do to relieve my symptoms? <Doctor> I'm sorry you're dealing with chronic heartburn. If antacids and dietary changes are not providing relief, it's essential to explore other treatment options. Please schedule an appointment with us so we can assess your condition, discuss these treatment options, and determine the best approach for managing your heartburn. ||
